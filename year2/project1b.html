Project 1b

For project 1b we worked as a group to design a masterplan responding to the existing Plymouth Council plan. This included a 4* hotel, over 80 residential units, and a series of cultural buildings that draw the public through the site. For my individual work I chose to design a swimming pool and café as part of the 4* hotel, alongside the art gallery and studio as one of the cultural buildings. I chose to link the two programmes to interrupt the direct path across the masterplan and draw the public into my programmes. (Images not to scale).

 

CAD drawing of the group masterplan overlaid onto a map of West Hoe, Plymouth. The red lines are an example of one of the design action diagrams we drew to help finalise the masterplan.

To show a range of skills, we made a digital model in 123D Design of the masterplan. Also we used the CAD drawing of the masterplan to create a laser cut model at scale 1:200, we made the physical model to better represent the steps leading up to main public space on our site.

In my individual design work I made a digital model of the structure of my proposal. I created this in 123D Design.

For the technology component of this submission I used my digital model to capture axonometrics and added layers in photoshop to show the technical layers within my roof.

This is a section of my building drawn in CAD and overlaid in Photoshop to add atmosphere. The section cuts through my whole proposal showing a part of each programme I was designing; a swimming pool with overlooking café, art gallery with studio above for local artists.

From my digital model I also captured a perspective and overlaid in Photoshop materiality to show the atmosphere when walking through my proposal.
