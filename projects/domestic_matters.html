Domestic Matters

This project begins with research into the West Hoe site and precedent of Barbican Estate, London. From initial research we designed a single person dwelling, my design breaks the boundary that existed between the residential and leisure areas of West Hoe, Plymouth. Developing from this we worked as a group to design a masterplan for the Quality Inn site. The masterplan included a 4* hotel, over 80 residential units and a series of cultural buildings to draw the public in. I chose to design an art gallery and studio as one of the cultural buildings, connected to the swimming pool and caf√© that was part of the 4* hotel.
