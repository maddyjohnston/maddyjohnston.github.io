Tavistock

Project 6 of first year was the culmination of all we had learnt throughout first year. The project involved site analysis of the materials that were present within our site in Tavistock, Devon. As Tavistock is an UNESCO world heritage site I chose to design a museum, with space on the first floor for an academic researching the area to stay temporarily. All final work was hand drawn at a variety of scales.
